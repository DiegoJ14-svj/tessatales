Tessa's Terrific Tales

An 8-Book Adventure Series for Young Inventors

Meet Tessa!

Join Tessa, a brilliant young inventor, as she navigates middle school with her amazing gadgets, loyal friends Leo and Maya, and a knack for turning everyday problems into extraordinary adventures!

Inspired by the beloved Diary of a Wimpy Kid style, these books combine humor, heart, and the magic of invention in stories that will captivate young readers.

Meet the Characters

Tessa

A brilliant young inventor with a knack for turning everyday problems into extraordinary adventures. She's creative, determined, and always ready with a new gadget!

Leo

Tessa's logical and methodical best friend. He helps debug her inventions and keeps her grounded with his practical approach to problem-solving.

Maya

The artistic soul of the group, Maya brings creativity and visual flair to Tessa's inventions. She sees beauty in chaos and inspiration everywhere.

About the Series

Tessa's Terrific Tales is an 8-book series that combines the humor and relatability of Diary of a Wimpy Kid with the excitement of invention and discovery. Each book follows Tessa as she navigates the challenges of middle school, friendship, and growing up, all while creating amazing (and sometimes chaotic) inventions.

The series teaches valuable lessons about:

  • Problem-solving and creative thinking
  • The importance of friendship and teamwork
  • Learning from mistakes and failures
  • Building confidence and finding your unique talents
  • The joy of invention and discovery

Perfect for young readers who love humor, adventure, and stories about kids who think outside the box!
